About Us

We help you to identify the media playing around you, explore the music you love.

We are providing a cloud based service to sample an audio clip using a PC or smartphone’s microphone and
identify the song by comparing with a cloud based music repository.

What's that tune helps a casual listener or a hardcore audiophile, or just a person sitting in a café and
wondering what’s playing on the speakers, this service is for anyone who wants to identify an
audio clip without having to download an app or having to fill pages of registration info.

We help you to identify songs with accuracy, and in case of reasonable doubt, provide a list of best matches
without compromising on the audio sample size and making the operation heavy.
